Troop Training and Promotion Guide

Troop Training vs. Promotion

The first priority is to ensure you have enough troops to fill all your marches. Training new troops is the only way to increase troop count

Once that is satisified, you should try to promote up lower troops. This has a number of benefits:

  • There is a small power benefit to promoting lower troops over training new ones. It only applies up to three levels below your current level. For example, if you can train T9 troops then you get a boost for promoting T6 and below. T7 and T8 promotion give the same power as training new T9, so you don't lose anything here, you just don't get the boost
  • When you fight, your lower troops will get injured first. If they fill up your infirmary then when your higher level troops get injured, they will die

Promotion is valuable during events like Hall of Governors, as you can get many more promoted in a single pass than trained from scratch

Troop Composition and Formations

  • General PvP: 5/2/3 or 6/2/2 (Infantry/Cavalry/Archers).
  • Defense: 5/4/1 for sturdy defensive setups.
  • Bear Trap Events: 1/1/10 to maximize archer damage.
