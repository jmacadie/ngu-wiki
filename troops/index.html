Troop Training and Promotion Guide

The number 1 rule is always be training. Training anything is better than having any of those buildings sitting idle

The number 2 rule is promote troops over train new. There are a number of reasons for this:

  • You lose nothing by promoting over training new. The power increase is the same and the training time is the same (accounting for the power added per training time). If that sounds too complex, don't worry, the main thing is that you don't lose out on power by promoting.
  • Weak troops will get injured first, fill up your infirmary and then when your strong troops get injured later they will die if the infirmary is already full of weak troops
  • Strong troops farm more effciently that weak troops, so while farming is an ok use of weak troops, you can get more done with fewer strong troops
  • Having weak troops means you are liable to fill rallies or garrison with those weak troops. There is only so much space on rallies, so if you fill out the spaces with poor troops you compromise the overall effectiveness. This doesn't always matter but for things like the Castle battle or Sanctuary battles, it can mean the difference between succeding or not

That's it. Simples!
