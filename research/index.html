Research Planner

This page comes in three parts

1️⃣ At the top of the page, you can use the filters to show a list of researches in the first table below. Use this to record what your current research levels are. The buttons are useful to bulk MAX a whole load of researches at the same time

2️⃣ Next there is a Stats section. In this section, input your research speed (from the Academy details) and your current academy level. With this, the page will calculate three different tables:

  • a table summarising what you have researched to date (the stats of which should be the same as the summary table you can pull up in the academy)
  • a table summarising the outstanding research at your current academy level
  • a further table that shows you the full horror of the 10 years research you have ahead of you
All the times shown are in your current research speed, as you continue to research 'Tool Enhancement' these times will come down

3️⃣ Finally, there is a recommended next research section ... or there will be! I've not built it yet ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
